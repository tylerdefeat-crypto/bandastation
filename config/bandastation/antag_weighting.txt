## WEIGHTED ANTAGONIST SELECTION SYSTEM ##

## Enable weighted antagonist selection.
#ANTAG_WEIGHTED_SELECTION

## Time window in days for tracking antagonist role history.
## Players who have taken antagonist roles within this window will have reduced selection weight.
ANTAG_HISTORY_WINDOW_DAYS 14

## Base weight for all candidates.
## This is the starting weight before any penalties are applied.
ANTAG_BASE_WEIGHT 100

## Weight penalty per recent antagonist role assignment.
## Each time a player was an antagonist in the time window, their weight is reduced by this amount.
ANTAG_WEIGHT_PENALTY 10

## Minimum weight a candidate can have.
## This prevents complete exclusion - even frequent antagonists have a small chance.
## Setting this too LOW may make frequent antags feel excluded.
## Setting this too HIGH reduces the effectiveness of the weighting system.
ANTAG_MIN_WEIGHT 1

## List of antagonists whose activity should be tracked for roundstart selection.
## These roles will count toward the penalty.
TRACKED_ANTAGONISTS_ROUNDSTART /datum/antagonist/traitor

## List of antagonists whose activity should be tracked for midround selection.
## These roles will count toward the penalty.
TRACKED_ANTAGONISTS_MIDROUND /datum/antagonist/blob

## List of antagonists whose activity should be tracked for latejoin selection.
## These roles will count toward the penalty.
TRACKED_ANTAGONISTS_LATEJOIN /datum/antagonist/traitor

## Log candidate weight calculations to the server log.
## Useful for debugging and monitoring the weighting system.
#LOG_ANTAG_CANDIDATE_WEIGHT
